who i am

Hello there, I am Ian. I am a versatile software engineer that has a strong hunger for business and entrepreneurship. To understand what I like and what I want I need to quickly iterate over my career

I started my career back in 2012 as one of the first full time employees at a really cool Norwegian startup in Oslo, named SocialCee. As the team grew at SocialCee during the following 4 years, I grew along with the company from being a Developer, to a team lead and in the end to the Head of Development.

During this journey I was lucky to be working with some very inspiring people that allowed me to be involved with many aspects of the building and growing of a company going from the development, devops, involvement in product management, defining our vision, communicating with important partners and getting a thorough understanding of our business model. The service of SocialCee is oriented at online marketing with a speciality in Social Media which helped me to have a good comprehension of this field.

Along this Journey I got infected with the entrepreneur virus so in Autumn 2016 I decided to give it a shot myself with a few friends to launch Nexpat.
